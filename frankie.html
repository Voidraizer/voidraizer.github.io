In Loving Memory

Franklin (Frankie) Lutikoff

October 27, 1989 - September 21, 2024

Loving husband, devoted son, and caring brother, Frankie is survived by his wife Sherry, his parents Lisa and Pete, his brother Steve, and his sister Lauren.
Above all else, Frankie cherished his family and eagerly looked forward to every opportunity to spend time with them.
He loved his life in Seattle with Sherry. But each Christmas, he traveled across the country from Seattle to Akron to enjoy a week or more of precious family time.
These are the memories that will be forever cherished by those he left behind.

Beyond the people in his life, Frankie was also the proud papa of three beloved rescue cats: Latte, Kelly, and Luna.
These cats brought him constant joy and will undoubtedly feel his absence.
While Frankie and Sherry enjoyed traveling to many exciting destinations, Frankie was truly happiest when returning home to relax and spend time with his beloved kitties.

Frankie was an accomplished software engineer and technical lead for the rideshare company Lyft, where he helped provide for his family.
He took great pride in the accomplishments of his team, especially their contributions to the backend payroll applications.
He was a graduate of Case Western Reserve University in Cleveland which found him his first job working at Philips Imaging
It was at Philips where Frankie met Sherry and had his life changed forever.
